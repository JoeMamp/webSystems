Hexed!

by Sustenance Center

Emilie Hoy, Ryan Waddell, Colton Zecca, Yaseen Mahmoud, Joe Mampillil

Directions:

The goal of hexed is to guess the RGB values for a shape of a given color. Move the sliders to adjust the color displayed in the shape, and click the "Got it!" button when you think you've got it. You can select the game's difficulty and the number of turns you have to customize the Hexed! experience to your liking.
